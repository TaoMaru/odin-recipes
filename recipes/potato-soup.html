Potato Soup Recipe

This wonderful, creamy soup is hearty and soothing.

This is the one soup that's a pleasure to eat year-round!

Ingredients (Ok, it's the same as the Bread Recipe)

  • Flour 500g
  • Water 270g
  • Salt 8g
  • Yeast 7g

Instructions (Yep, it's all just a copy-paste)

  1. Add flour, salt, yeast to medium bowl and whisk a bit.
  2. Add water to stand mixer bowl and switch mixer on to lowest speed.
  3. Add dry mix to water one heaping tablespoon at a time.
  4. Set mixer to appropriate kneading speed. Let knead 10 minutes.
  5. Turn dough out onto smooth surface, shape into ball.
  6. Place ball into oiled bowl, cover with cloth. Rest 1 hour.
  7. Turn dough out onto smooth surface, knead a bit. Shape into loaf.
  8. Place loaf onto greased baking sheet, cover with cloth. Rest 1 hour.
  9. With sharp blade, score top of loaf 4 or more times as needed.
  10. Place in preheated oven at 395 degrees F. Bake 25 min.
  11. Remove from oven. Let cool on cooling rack at least 30 minutes. Enjoy.
